W4T Labs

We are a BC-based high-tech research lab

W4T Labs is a registered high-tech enterprise in British Columbia, Canada. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of science through deep research in cryptography, AI diagnostics, and astrophycis, while profoundly committing to the education of the next generation.

OUR FOCUS​

What Drives Innovation

W4T Labs explores intersections between theoretical mathematics, artificial intelligence, and computational physics.

We Research​

Deepening the foundations of RSA cryptography and advanced mathematical frameworks.

We Compute

Leveraging distributed networks for complex protein structure predictions.

We Diagnose​

Pioneering AI-assisted medical diagnostics to improve early healthcare outcomes.

We Discover​

Analyzing massive astrophysical datasets to uncover the secrets of the universe.

Through advanced computational methods and rigorous academic research, we strive to push the boundaries of human knowledge

AI-Assisted Medical Diagnostics

Our project focuses on 24-hour ECG data collection and subsequent AI-driven diagnosis. A review of the EBSCO academic database confirms that our data collection methodology is currently at the forefront of the field, providing unprecedented insights for early heart disease detection.

Protein Structure Prediction

Through distributed computing, we actively contribute to the Rosetta@home project, developed by the Baker Lab at the University of Washington. Our experimental results advance research in protein structure prediction, protein-protein docking, and the Human Proteome Folding Project.

Nurturing Young Talent

At W4T Labs, we are profoundly committed to youth education. Our laboratory is proud to mentor exceptional young scholars, including champions of the Canadian Mathematics Competitions, school champions in the University of Waterloo’s Fryer and CIMC contests, and core members of FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) robotics championship-winning teams.
